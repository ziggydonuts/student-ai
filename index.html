About this project

Two AI tools built to solve real problems for Indian college students.

Study Buddy

AI · Education · Productivity

Problem: Students don't know how to break a subject into a study plan with limited time. Generic resources don't adapt to the person's level, time, or goal.

What it does: Paste any topic, select time, level, and goal — get a day-by-day study plan and 5-question quiz with instant feedback, tailored to your inputs.

  • Designed the input flow and weighting logic (time × level × goal)
  • Engineered the prompt to produce structured JSON output (plan + quiz)
  • Built and deployed the full frontend with tab navigation and quiz scoring

ScholarBot

AI · Social Impact · First-Gen Students

Problem: Thousands of scholarships exist for Indian students but most first-generation college students don't know what they're eligible for. The NSP portal is overwhelming.

What it does: A conversational AI that takes your background (state, caste, income, course) and recommends specific scholarships you qualify for with eligibility criteria and application steps.

  • Identified the core user problem through conversations with first-gen students
  • Designed the conversation flow and system prompt with scholarship knowledge
  • Built the full chat interface and deployed as part of this portfolio
Built as part of an application to IIM Ahmedabad Summer Residency Program · 2025
